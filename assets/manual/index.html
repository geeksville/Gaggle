What is Gaggle?

Gaggle is a tool for hang-glider, paraglider and sailplane pilots.

Eventually this application will allow your Android phone to fill most of requirements of a flight GPS. It is still early days, but already many logging and useful flight instruments are included. As a beta tester of this application, your feedback is especially valuable - please visit the support forum and post your requests/feedback.

Warning: This application is still in beta testing, you should not make potientially life threatening decisions based on its information.
