What is Gaggle?

Gaggle is a GPS & Vario for hang-glider, paraglider and sailplane pilots.

The GPL v3 license is included in this distribution in the /assets/manual/ folder. More information can be found online at http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.txt

The ambition is to turn this into a fully-fledged flight GPS. As a user of this application, your feedback is especially valuable - please visit the support forum and post your requests/feedback.

Warning: You should not make potentially life threatening decisions based on the information provided by this application

As the pilot, you are responsible for your safety. This application can help you guide that decision but you should always treat the information provided with care.
